Got Questions?

Common Questions

Does it sound robotic?

No. Modern AI voice technology uses natural-sounding voices with appropriate pacing, tone, and conversational patterns. Most callers find it indistinguishable from a helpful human assistant.

What if the caller asks something unexpected?

The agent is trained to handle your specific business scenarios. If it encounters something outside its scope, it gracefully transfers to a human team member with a full transcript of the conversation.

How long does implementation take?

Typically 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to launch, depending on the complexity of your custom conversation flows and system integrations.

Can it integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. We integrate with most CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.), booking systems (Calendly, Acuity), and phone providers (Twilio, RingCentral).
